**数据库检查**:
```sql
SELECT id, case_no, record_type, LENGTH(content) as content_length 
FROM inpatient_records 
ORDER BY created_at DESC 
LIMIT 4;
```

- ✅ 每条记录的 record_type 正确（admission/progress/discharge/operation）
